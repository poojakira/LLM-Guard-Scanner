# Limitations Documentation

## Known Limitations
1. **False Positives**: Complex linguistic structures may trigger false positives in injection detection.
2. **Latency**: High-perplexity scanning adds overhead to real-time inference.
3. **Model Specificity**: Some detectors are optimized for GPT-style architectures and may underperform on others.
4. **Context Window**: Scans are limited to the input context window and may miss cross-session threats.
